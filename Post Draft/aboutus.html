About Us

The Crafting Collective is an online resource for fiber crafts. We provide tutorials, patterns, and other information that crafts may need. Currently we focus on knitting, crochet, embroidery, quilting, and general sewing crafts. We also have extended resources and further resources.

The Crafting Collective was created by the New England Library Alliance (NELA). NELA serves a high number of users and patrons that are interested in fiber crafts. Due to this, NELA wanted to create an online resource for its users to access craft resources whenever they needed and enable them to find the information they wanted.

The Crafting Collective is currently only presented in English, but NELA does have future plans to expand the language options for their New England Patrons. This and offering more crafting resources are the future goals of the crating collective as this site grows to be a resource hub for fabric crafts pointing users to online resources, free patterns, and informational material for every level of crafter.
